Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a bill?

Adding a bill is simple:

  • Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  • Choose to take a photo, import from gallery, or enter manually
  • The app will automatically extract bill details using OCR
  • Review and confirm the information
  • Tap "Save" to add the bill
Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority:

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No cloud storage or servers involved
  • We never see or access your bill information
  • No tracking, no analytics, no data collection
  • Optional biometric lock (Face ID/Touch ID) for extra security

Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How accurate is the OCR scanning?

Our OCR technology is highly accurate (90%+ success rate), but results depend on:

  • Photo Quality: Good lighting and focus improve accuracy
  • Bill Format: Clear, printed text works best
  • Language: Optimized for English text

Tip: Always review extracted information before saving. You can easily edit any field if needed.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

For iOS (iPhone/iPad):

  • Open Settings app
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap "Subscriptions"
  • Select BillSnap Premium
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription"

For Android:

  • Open Google Play Store
  • Tap Menu → Subscriptions
  • Select BillSnap Premium
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I backup my data?

Premium users can export data:

  • Go to Settings in the app
  • Tap "Export Data"
  • Choose a location to save the backup file
  • The file includes all bill data (JSON format)

To restore from backup:

  • Go to Settings → "Import Data"
  • Select your backup file
  • Confirm the import

Free users: Upgrade to Premium to access backup features, or manually note down your important bills.

I'm not receiving bill reminders. What should I do?

Check these settings:

  • App Permissions: Ensure BillSnap has notification permission in device settings
  • Do Not Disturb: Make sure your device isn't in Do Not Disturb mode
  • Due Date: Reminders are sent 3 days before the due date
  • Battery Optimization: Disable battery optimization for BillSnap (Android)
  • Background App Refresh: Enable for BillSnap (iOS)

If issues persist, contact us at help@billsnap.online

Can I use BillSnap on multiple devices?

Currently, BillSnap stores all data locally on each device:

  • Premium Subscription: Can be used on multiple devices with the same Apple ID or Google account
  • Data Sync: Not currently available (data stays on each device)
  • Workaround: Use Export/Import to manually transfer data between devices

Cloud sync is a feature we're considering for future updates!

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

Important: All your bill data and photos will be permanently deleted when you uninstall BillSnap.

  • Data is stored locally on your device only
  • We don't have backup copies (privacy-first design)
  • Before uninstalling: Export your data if you're a Premium user
  • Take screenshots of important bills as backup
Do you support multiple currencies?

Yes! BillSnap supports various currencies:

  • USD ($), EUR (€), GBP (£), CAD ($), AUD ($), and more
  • Set your preferred currency in Settings
  • OCR automatically detects currency symbols
  • Manual entry supports any currency
Can I categorize my bills?

Yes! BillSnap includes common bill categories:

  • Electric
  • Water
  • Gas
  • Internet
  • Phone
  • Cable/TV
  • Rent/Mortgage
  • Insurance
  • Credit Card
  • Other (custom)

Assign categories when adding or editing bills for better organization.
